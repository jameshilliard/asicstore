Block Erupter Cube

Speed: 30 GH/s guaranteed. (38.4 GH/s theoretical when overclocked)
Power Consumption: Approx. 200W. (Approx. 280W when overclocked)
Compact, self-contained unit: 135.5mm x 152.5mm x 145mm

Plug & Play

2PCIe 6 pin connectors for power supply.
Rj45 socket for Ethernet connection.
USB Connector for debugging and firmware updates.
Configuration through web page.
Status LED indicating getwork/putwork activity.

Powered by the World's First Mining ASIC.

Consists of six mini blades, each with 16 ASIC chips.
Speed of each chip could be monitored in web page.
Overclocking just by one button click.

Efficient Cooling

Tubular structure built for airflow.
Built-in 12cm fan, strong but quiet.
Protection: the unit will be powered off in case of fan failure or slow fan.

Scalable

Extruded aluminum enclosure. Rigid and tough.
Multiple units to be slided into each other by "tongue and groove" system, allowing for integrated larger settings.
Accessaries coming soon for deployment in scales: adapter for server PSUs, cable converters, etc.
